# My resume/CV

For the past few years I've worked primarily in data platforms, though my first jobs in tech were in full-stack web development in PHP/Jquery/Wordpress/other monstrosities. I've done a lot of things, though you probably don't want me to write PHP or Wordpress anymore. If you'd like to get in touch, see the [about page](/about) on this site.

### Jobs (Things that paid me)

* 🏦💵 **[Anrok](https://anrok.com) - _July 2015 -> Present_**
  * I currently work at Anrok, a series B sales tax compliance startup, where I joined as their first data platform hire.
* 🏴🏳️ **[LaunchDarkly](https://launchdarkly.com/) - _October 2021 -> July 2025_**
  * I was an early hire on LaunchDarkly's internal data team and helped grow that team 3x and mature our internal analytics. I later moved to the core data platform team to work the streaming and microbatch data platforms powering pretty much anything in the LaunchDarkly platform that relies on data.
* ✈️💸 **[Yapta -> Coupa Software](https://www.geekwire.com/2020/coupa-software-acquiring-seattle-startup-yapta-help-businesses-cut-travel-costs/) - _November 2019 ->  October 2021_**
  * I joined a travel startup providing automated fare repricing to thousands of customers' airfare and hotel business travel programs. Yapta was acquired right before the pandemic destroyed business travel, and after acquisition, I transitioned to working on the pipelines backing Coupa's embedded analytics product.
* 🚒🚑 **[Emergency Reporting](https://emergencyreporting.com/) - _March 2018 -> November 2019_**
  * I wrote SQL, PHP, Javascript, and SSRS reports for a company providing incident reporting software to fire departments and other public safety agencies.
* 🏨♿️ **[AbiliTrek](https://www.geekwire.com/2017/abilitrek-offers-hotel-database-travelers-disabilities-filling-information-void-17b-market/) - _April 2017 -> March 2018_**
  * I got my first web development job working mostly in PHP and MySQL for a startup aiming to make travel more accessible.
* 📚⚽️ **Various retail jobs**
  * They paid the bills in college.
