# My resume/CV

For the past few years I've worked primarily in data platforms and infrastructure, though my first jobs in tech were in full-stack web development in PHP, jQuery, and WordPress. Prior to that, I taught myself to code by making little WordPress sites for myself. If you'd like to get in touch, see the [about page](/about) on this site.

### Jobs (Things that paid me)

* 📊🔎 **[Fieldguide](https://www.fieldguide.io/) - _April 2026 -> Present_**
  * I currently work on Fieldguide's infra platform team, where I'm working on scaling our existing AWS infrastructure to multiple regions, clouds, and FedRAMP compliance.
* 🏦💵 **[Anrok](https://www.anrok.com/) - _July 2025 -> April 2026_**
  * I was the first (and for a while only) data hire at Anrok, a sales tax compliance startup. My work was focused on standing up a foundational data platform and improving our observability infrastructure as the company scaled.
* 🏴🏳️ **[LaunchDarkly](https://launchdarkly.com/) - _October 2021 -> July 2025_**
  * I was an early hire on LaunchDarkly's internal data team and helped grow that team 3x while maturing and stabilizing our internal analytics. I later moved to the core data platform team to work on the streaming and microbatch data platforms powering pretty much anything in the LaunchDarkly platform that relies on data.
* ✈️💸 **[Yapta -> Coupa Software](https://www.coupa.com/newsroom/coupa-acquires-travel-price-optimization-leader-yapta/) - _November 2019 -> October 2021_**
  * I worked on the data platform for a startup providing automated fare repricing to thousands of customers' air and hotel business travel programs. Yapta was acquired right before the pandemic cut our business 85%, and after acquisition, I transitioned to working on the data platform backing Coupa's embedded analytics product.
* 🚒🚑 **[Emergency Reporting](https://emergencyreporting.com/) - _March 2018 -> November 2019_**
  * I wrote SQL, PHP, Javascript, and SSRS reports for a company providing incident reporting software to fire departments and other public safety agencies.
* 🏨♿️ **[AbiliTrek](https://www.geekwire.com/2017/abilitrek-offers-hotel-database-travelers-disabilities-filling-information-void-17b-market/) - _April 2017 -> March 2018_**
  * I got my first web development job working mostly in PHP and MySQL for a startup aiming to make travel more accessible.
* 📚⚽️ **Various retail jobs**
  * They paid the bills in college.
